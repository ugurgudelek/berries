
The success of convolutional neural networks in the field of computer vision attracted the attention of the researchers from other fields. One of the research areas in which neural networks is actively used is financial forecasting. In this paper, we propose a novel method for predicting stock price movements using CNN. We extract commonly used trend indicators and momentum indicators from financial time series data and use these as our features. Adopting a sliding window approach, we generate our images by taking snapshots that are bounded by the window over a daily period. We then perform daily predictions, namely, regression for predicting the stock prices and classification for predicting the movement of the prices on the next day, which can be modified to estimate weekly or monthly trends. To increase the number of images, we use numerous stocks. Finally, we evaluate our method by performing virtual trading and calculating the final capital. We also compare our method's performance to commonly used classical trading strategies and feed-forward neural networks. Our results show that we can predict the next day's prices with 78% accuracy and end up with 7:1 of our initial capital, taking realistic values of transaction costs into account.
