		GGAP-RBF network was also applied to predict stock prices and achieve good prediction accuracy (Wang, Huang, Saratchandran, & Sundararajan, 2005)

		A new approach based on logistic regression model predicted the stock price trend of next month by using the stock prices of current month (Gong & Sun, 2009).
